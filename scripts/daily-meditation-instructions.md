# Daily Meditation Instructions (v2.3)

## Schedule
Every day at 04:00 (Asia/Shanghai), reviewing **yesterday**.

## Responsibilities
The meditation job is the **review and insight production entrypoint**.

It should:
- review yesterday's work
- extract problems, improvements, and follow-up plans
- produce new insight material for future memory maintenance
- publish a diary / summary if desired

It should **not** directly update `MEMORY.md`.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: review yesterday's daily memory
Read the dated daily-memory file for the reviewed day.

Example:
- if the job runs at 04:00 on March 10
- it reviews March 9
- and reads `memory/2026-03-09.md`

### Step 2: generate insight material
If new high-value reflections appear during review, write them back into the **corresponding dated daily-memory file**.

Example:
- running on March 10 at 04:00
- write back to `memory/2026-03-09.md`

Do **not** copy high-score items directly into `MEMORY.md`.

### Step 3: write lessons and improvements
Update learning files as needed.

### Step 4: publish diary / summary
Optional, depending on your deployment.

---

## Important rules

- all review work targets **yesterday**, not today
- do not bypass the maintenance pipeline
- new insight material should flow back into dated daily memory first
- later maintenance cycles will score and consolidate it formally
